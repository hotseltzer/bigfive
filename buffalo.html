Handsome Buffalo

The African Buffalo, a capable swimmer and unpredictable animal, have few predators aside from humans and lions, and can defend themselves against both. They are regarded as dangerous animals because wounded buffalo do not forget and will attack and gore their hunters, leading to the nickname "widowmaker." The African Buffalo is a sought-after hunting trophy with some hunters paying $10000 for the opportunity to hunt them. There is currently no huge poaching threat to the buffalo population and the International Union for Conservation of Nature has listed the African Buffalo as a Least Concern
