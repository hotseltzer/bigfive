Majestic Lion

The Lion is the only big cat that live in groups, called prides. Prides are close family groups that work together to hunt and defend their territory. The female lionesse is the better hunter and the male lion protects the pride. Male lions are recognizable by their distinctive mane and those with darker manes are more attractive to lionesses. Lions are somewhat lazy and don't like to exert a lot of enegry, resting for 20 hours a day. Hearing a lion roar in the wild is exhilirating and terrifying at the same time!
