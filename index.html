The 'Big Five'- lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo and rhino are five of South Africa's amazing wild animals. The term 'Big Five', orginally used by hunters, is now synonymous with African safaris. A trip to South Africa is not complete until all five animals have been spotted and photographed.
