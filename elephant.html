Powerful Elephant

The Elephant is the world's largest land animal and they LOVE playing in water. An elephant's skin is extremely sentivtive, they can feel a fly land on them. They protect their skin with regular mud baths. Their 400 pound trunk is so dexterous it can pick up a single grain of rice. Elephants communicate with each other through touch. They greet each other by stroking and wrapping their trunks and older elephants discipline younger ones with trunk-slaps, kicks and shoves. Elephants are avid eaters, feeding for up to 16 hours daily and consuming up to 600 pounds of food. The world's population of elephants has dramatically decreased as a result of poaching and habitat destruction. Elephants are poached for their tusks.
